## Policy vs practice

Airlines publish rules, but enforcement can vary by:
- route and aircraft type
- gate congestion
- staff discretion
- priority tiering and fare class

## Why variability exists

Carry-on enforcement is often a response to operational constraints:
- overhead bin capacity
- boarding speed
- safety requirements

When constraints tighten, enforcement increases.

## A better mental model

Treat carry-on limits as a capacity management system, not a moral rule.
