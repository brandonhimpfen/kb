## Buffer time is protection against uncertainty

Airports are multi-queue systems:
- check-in and bag drop
- security screening
- immigration and document checks
- gate changes and walking time

The buffer is a hedge against variance across these queues.

## Why solo travelers experience buffers differently

When you travel alone, there is less redundancy:
- one person handles all steps
- a single delay compounds more directly

## Practical interpretation

A buffer is not a “rule.” It is an **uncertainty budget**.
