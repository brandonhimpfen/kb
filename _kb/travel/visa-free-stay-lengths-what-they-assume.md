## “Visa-free” is not the same as “permission for anything”

Visa-free typically describes:
- the ability to enter without a visa sticker
- for a specific purpose (often tourism)
- for a maximum duration

It does not automatically grant work permission, residency rights, or multiple-entry privileges.

## Duration is rarely the only condition

Even when a duration is stated, entry can depend on:
- proof of onward travel
- financial means
- purpose clarity
- passport validity

## Why this matters for solo travelers

Solo travelers often face more direct questioning at borders because they do not have a shared itinerary or companion context.
