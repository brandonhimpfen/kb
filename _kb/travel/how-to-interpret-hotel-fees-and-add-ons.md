## Fees are often a separate pricing channel

Hotels may separate revenue into:
- base rate (what ranking engines compare)
- mandatory fees (resort, destination, service)
- taxes (often local)
- optional add-ons (breakfast, late checkout)

## Why it matters for solo travelers

Solo travelers often compare on total cost more tightly. Fee structures can distort comparisons and make “cheap” rooms expensive.

## What to look for

A good mental model is to classify each line item as:
- mandatory
- conditional
- optional
