## The rule in one sentence

You can spend **up to 90 days** in the Schengen Area in **any rolling 180-day period**.

The phrase “rolling” is the part that confuses people.

## Why “rolling” matters

It means the window is always moving. At any date you check, you look back 180 days and count how many days you were present.

## What counts as a day

In most interpretations, **any day you are present** counts as a day, including partial days.

## How to think about it

Instead of asking “How long can I stay this trip?” ask:

> “How many Schengen days have I used in the last 180 days?”
