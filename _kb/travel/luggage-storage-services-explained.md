## Storage exists because travel timing does not line up neatly

Check-out, check-in, train departures, museum visits, and airport transfers often operate on different clocks.

Luggage storage services exist to bridge that mismatch.

## Why this matters more for solo travelers

When traveling alone, your bag is often your whole operating base. Carrying it everywhere increases friction, limits spontaneity, and can make routine tasks more tiring.

## The real function

Bag storage is not only about convenience. It is a way of temporarily separating movement from possessions so that the hours between arrival and departure remain usable.
