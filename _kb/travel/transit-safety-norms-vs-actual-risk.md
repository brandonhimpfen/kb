## Normal is not the same as safe

A behavior can be common and still be risky. Norms are shaped by local habits, infrastructure, enforcement, and social context.

## Why solo travelers feel the difference

When traveling alone, you have fewer buffers:
- no second opinion in the moment
- fewer shared responsibilities
- higher impact of small errors

## What to treat as signals

Signals are not “rules,” but they can indicate elevated risk:
- isolation and low visibility
- cash-only pressure in transit
- mismatched identity checks in rideshare

The goal is calibration, not fear.
