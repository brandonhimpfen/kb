# AI in Software Development

Artificial intelligence (AI) refers to computer systems designed to perform tasks that typically require human intelligence. 
These tasks can include recognizing patterns, understanding language, making predictions, and solving complex problems.

## Why This Topic Matters

AI technologies are increasingly embedded in everyday systems such as search engines, recommendation platforms, 
software development tools, and data analysis systems. Understanding how AI works helps individuals and organizations 
evaluate its capabilities, limitations, and impact.

## Key Concepts

- Algorithms and models used to process information
- Data used to train AI systems
- Evaluation methods to measure performance
- Ethical considerations such as bias and fairness

## Applications

Artificial intelligence is used in areas such as:

- Software development and automation
- Data analysis and decision support
- Natural language processing and conversational systems
- Computer vision and image analysis
- Research and scientific discovery
