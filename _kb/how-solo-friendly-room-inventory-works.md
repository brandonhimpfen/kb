## “Limited” usually means a real constraint

Inventory can be limited because:
- the property has few true single rooms
- the operator only holds a small allotment
- the offer is tied to a specific room category

## Why this affects planning

Solo travelers often need predictability earlier. When solo inventory is constrained, the planning window shrinks.

## A useful interpretation

When you see “limited solo rooms,” interpret it as:
- a capacity constraint
- not a promise
- not a universal property feature
