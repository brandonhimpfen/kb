## What advisories are designed to do

They are designed to:
- communicate risks and disruptions
- set expectations for assistance
- outline restrictions and entry requirements

They are not designed to rank destinations by “safety.”

## What to look for

Useful details often include:
- affected regions (not just the country name)
- specific triggers (strikes, elections, storms)
- what changes in service availability

## A stable approach

Treat official sources as:
- baseline context
- not a real-time feed
- not a substitute for local situational awareness
