## Confidence is often evidence-based

Many people interpret confidence as a feeling you must have first. In practice, it often emerges as a conclusion:

> “I handled that.”

Solo travel produces repeated moments where you must move forward with incomplete certainty. When those moments resolve, confidence accumulates.

## Why this matters for a knowledge base

A strong reference layer reduces uncertainty, which reduces the emotional cost of acting.

Systems can support psychology.
