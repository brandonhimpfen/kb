## Legitimacy is usually signaled, not claimed

Legitimate transport is usually identifiable through:
- official queues or stands
- visible licensing or identifiers
- matching details (plate, name, vehicle type)

## The key failure mode is mismatch

Most problems start when something does not match:
- the driver identity
- the vehicle plate
- the pickup flow inside the app

## Why this matters

Verification is not about distrust. It is about reducing unnecessary uncertainty when traveling alone.
