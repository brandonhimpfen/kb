## The solo difference is the absence of delegation

When you travel with someone else, micro-decisions are distributed:
- route choices
- meal timing
- safety judgments
- budget trade-offs

When alone, they accumulate in one mind.

## Why it matters

Decision fatigue can lead to:
- overpaying to end decisions quickly
- avoiding new experiences
- reduced patience for friction

## A stable counterweight

Systems reduce fatigue. That is one reason this Knowledge Base exists.
